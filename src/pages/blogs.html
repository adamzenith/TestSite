Blogs I enjoy

Scott Alexander

One of the great bloggers. Has in depth articles on a variety of topics spanning healthcare, rationality, politics, spirituality, and really anything else you can think of. Also hosts yearly book review(or not a book reviews), that draw really high quality submissions

Zvi Mowshowitz

At the moment covers all(and I mean all) developments in the AI space. When progress slows enough, also covers US housing, childcare and rationality adjacent topics. Skimming his AI posts are possibly the best way to stay up to date on the topic!

Matt Lakeman

Travels around the world and writes in depth commentaries on local histories, with one of my favorites being the one on El Salvador and their Mano Dura policy. He also does book "extractions", where he tries to extract the essence of books in shorter form, and write-ups of random experiments he chooses to do, like having his eyes covered for 24 hours.
