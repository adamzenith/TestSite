Explainable Brains Hackathon

2026 · source

This project was a 2.5 hour hackathon, with the goal of simplifying the task of labelling activations in the brains of mice when treated with ozempic. Currently all labelling is manual, and Vibraint wants to train a labelling model.

Our final product was a tool that can take a bunch of unlabelled patches of these brainscans, and cluster them in a way that highlights different patch types, so the manual labelling will cover as broad a subset of the data as possible. This should improve the performace of the labelling model, at minimal cost in human labelling hours.
