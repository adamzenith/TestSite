Explainable Brains Hackathon

2026 · Github

This project was a 2.5 hour hackathon, with the goal of simplifying the task of labeling activations in the brains of mice when treated with ozempic. Currently all labeling is manual, and Vibraint wants to train a labeling model.

Our final product was a tool that can take a bunch of unlabelled patches of these brain scans, and cluster them in a way that highlights different patch types, so the manual labeling will cover as broad a subset of the data as possible. This should improve the performance of the labeling model, at minimal cost in human labeling hours.

There were over 40 contestants, and we walked away from it with a first prize!
